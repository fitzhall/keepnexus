## Philosophy Applied

### **Elon's Principles ‚Üí Our Implementation**

1. **"Question every requirement"**
   - Removed: Animations, gradients, icons, fancy effects
   - Kept: Only what's needed to understand and manage rules

2. **"Delete any part you can"**
   - No simulation mode
   - No drag-and-drop
   - No visual rule builder
   - Just forms that work

3. **"Simplify and optimize"**
   - 4 columns: WHO, CAN DO, WHEN, CONDITION
   - That's it. That's the whole system.

4. **"Accelerate cycle time"**
   - Click template ‚Üí Fill form ‚Üí Create
   - Under 30 seconds to create a rule

5. **"Automate"**
   - Rules execute themselves
   - No manual intervention needed
